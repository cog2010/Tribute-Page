Dr. Neil deGrasse Tyson

The Man Who Killed Pluto

Turning Points

  • 1958 Born October 5th to Sunchita Maria and Cyril deGrasse Tyson in the Bronx, New York
  • 1975 Dr. Tyson spends a day in Ithica, NY studing with mentor and famed astrophysicist Dr. Carl Sagan
  • 1980 Graduated with a BA in Physics from Harvard University
  • 1983 Recieved an MA in Astronomy from The University of Texas at Austin
  • 1988 Completed his MPhil in Astrophsics from Columbia Universityand began work on PhD in Astrophysics which he recieved 2 years later
  • 1994 Dr. Tyson joined the staff of the Hayden Planetarium, a place hugely influential to his love of the cosmos
  • 1996 He is named Director of the Planetarium, a position he still holds today
  • 2004 NASA awards him with the Distinguished Public Service Medal, the highest civilian honor given by the organization
  • 2006 After many years of disputing Pluto as the ninth planet in out solar system, the IAU agrees with Dr. Tyson and Pluto is demoted to a Dwarf Planet
  • 2009 Dr. Tyson began hosting a talk radio program named Star Talk. The show only lasted 13 weeks but was resurrected later as a popular weekly podcast featuring scientists and comedians.
